Wizzo is the easiest way to find like-minded people locally. Do you have a rare hobby? There are probably people around who enjoy it, too. Do you just want to find some fun people to hang out with? Create an event and see who joins!


Here are some tips to get the most out of Wizzo.

Don’t lurk! This isn’t a swiping app. If you don’t post anything and don’t join any events, you won’t meet anyone. If you aren’t ready to join an event, just make a post and see who responds.

Interest tags are a fundamental piece of how Wizzo lets you interact with others. When creating your profile, choose the interest tags that reflect your specific passions. As an example, you can add #Painting and drawing or the more detailed, #Watercolor painting.

Keep in mind that everything in Wizzo is public. Comments, posts, and events can be seen by anyone within 30 miles. Only private messages are kept from public view. Don’t post anything that you don’t want to be public.

Express yourself. Make your profile a reflection of your unique personality. Be sure to add a profile picture so people know who you are at events. Keep in mind, all photos are automatically checked for inappropriate content.

Please stay safe. Wizzo is a platform for connecting people but we are not responsible for the actions of its users. While it is possible to create events at a private residence, it is recommended to meet new people in public places.

We hope you meet amazing people with Wizzo!
