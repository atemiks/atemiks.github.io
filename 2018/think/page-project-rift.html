Rift

“Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Maecenas mattis tincidunt velit. Mauris tincidunt nisi quis blandit pellentesque.”

CLIENT NAME, JOB TITLE

Brand direction, new campaign, assets and thinking.

CHALLENGE

With an advanced and established cross channel marketing campaign already in place, RIFT, like so many brands had begun to experience advertising saturation, particularly on social media platforms, and sought to revitalise their campaigns to improve engagement.

As a high-growth organistion there was also a wider brand challenge to help solve, consistency. With frequent new starters to meet the demand for their services, sought was help in optimising the national tax refunds brand to ensure every member of the team was able to understand the integrity of the brand, it’s vision, values and voice.

DELIVERY

Understanding that any targeted advertising needs to of course first attract, draw desire and interest then prompt for action, a range of impactful creative ads were designed, then split-test across multiple channels and audiences. These took on board new thinking and related customer imagery to add ‘nudge’ points for each audience.

To solve the brand consistency challenge and to help RIFT find who they are today, an in-depth customer profile study was used to really help identity what customers related to, valued the most and what were the emotive factors are which lead decision-making. Conversely, time was taken to interview the marketing department and key customer facing personnel to fully understand the team’s brand perspective. With this insight, and seeing the commonality in values, a new brand vision was created, presenting a clear definition of the voice and values together in a concise working document.

OUTCOMES
  • Multiple digital ad campaigns
  • Brand confidence
  • Ongoing digital marketing support
