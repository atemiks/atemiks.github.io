CLIENT NAME, JOB TITLE

New brand horizons for the UK’s Top Ranked Merchant Navy Officer Cadetship Provider.

CHALLENGE

For over 30 years Chiltern Maritime has remained the UK’s foremost provider of Merchant Navy Officer Cadetships.

Despite its obvious and enviable success, Chiltern Maritime recognised that its weakest business asset was its brand strength. Having relied on its formidable reputation and service to date, and with a website ill-equipped to compete in the current digital age, we were engaged by Chiltern Maritime to oversee the upwardly mobile development of its brand and launch a website capable of inspiring a new generation of Merchant Navy Officers. Two key factors of the site’s future success lie in its ability to engage with cadets and sponsors plus attract a larger number of applications.

To create a stronger brand image, Chiltern Maritime wanted us to grow its reach and introduce not only the prospect of a career at sea to prospective cadets, but also to build brand awareness and grow new levels of applications as the top cadetship provider.

DELIVERY

Chiltern Maritime enrolled on our EnVisioning Programme and the team were very open-minded to change and undertaking a fresh approach. They embraced the nature of the programme to re-align the business’ appeal to prospective cadets and cadetship providers (maritime charities and commercial maritime operators).

Building our understanding through cadet and experienced staff interviews, we created a consistent picture showcasing how cadets research a career path as well as the decision process regarding the important (emotive) factors innately influencing their thinking. This valuable content helped us to shape the rebranding process and present clear identification of the vision, values and tone of voice for the brand.

From the EnVisioning process came not only the refreshed contemporary brand identity but a consistent aesthetic look to be rolled out in the digital phase.

For the Chiltern Maritime website, whilst still targeting cadetship sponsors, our foremost aim was to both satisfy and inspire prospective Deck, Engineering and Electro-Technical Officer Cadets. In shaping the UX, including the content and promotional messaging mix, the aim was to deliver strong marketing value by showing potential future sponsors how in-tune and aligned Chiltern Maritime is with cadets and its’ ability to attract and help select the best candidates. The general feel and look of the website is clean, visually led, informative and responsive, and reflects a company which is highly adaptable and versatile.

OUTCOMES
  • Hugely positive response from sponsors
  • High engagement response from cadets
  • Bounce rate down to under 2%
  • Traffic up year on year
