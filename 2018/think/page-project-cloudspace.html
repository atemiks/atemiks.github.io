CLIENT NAME, JOB TITLE

Brand transformation of a high-tech hosting business as it grows its international penetration.

CHALLENGE

Looking to reposition its brand to match its increasing international focus, cloud, dedicated and co-location hosting business, CloudSpace commissioned Think Studio to deliver creativity, thinking and corporate media.

The brief was to achieve a new level of distinctiveness and as a growing brand standout in what can be a homogenous marketplace. Important was to heighten its ‘value advantage’ appeal to a diverse customer base, some with simple requirements, others very bespoke. Customer research showed what customers appreciate most is the high-level of personalised customer service they receive 24/7/365.

In delivering the media, notably the website, the challenge was to forge a new identity that balanced personality to be distinctively attractive and interesting whilst also pitching enough technical and service detail to ensure credibility.

Beyond the delivery of the media, but still to be mindful of during design and build, was the introduction of new promotional campaigns across search and display, therefore strong measurement to optimise performance was imperative.

DELIVERY

Enrolling in Think’s EnVisioning Programme, input and insight was sought from CloudSpace’s customers, the customer service team and leadership helping clearly understand the underlying brand values and product attributes

The learning gained during the programme was exploited in the subsequent creative process as a new digital identity was created, one which is striking and engaging but which also delivers a high level of UX. The same care and level of attention was also placed across the deeper order pages and which are as effective on mobile as on larger resolutions.

OUTCOMES
  • Impactful, distinctive and memorable site
  • Website bounce rate down to under 10% (from 50%+)
  • Powerful campaign graphics
  • Highly targeted international display campaign
  • A Partner (Affiliate) Programme, building a referral and partnership base
