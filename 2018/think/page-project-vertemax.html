CLIENT NAME, JOB TITLE

International rebrand and digital makeover for leading workzone safety company.

CHALLENGE

As then, Tammet Systems (rebranded to Vertemax), were at a major crossroads in its business development strategy. As a highly successful company experiencing fast growth, they quickly came to the understanding of the urgent need for a brand that worked and could be protected globally and across markets. Reflecting on their now market leader status, to achieve the right positioning and to meet their growing international expansion needs, Tammet Systems also sought a strong digital presence, one which was scalable and met the varied user needs.

With ambitious plans to expand their operation, including growing the product range and developing new territories, a new level of marketing support was required to help deliver a more expansive strategy across new channels and targeting greater customer engagement.

DELIVERY

Engaged on Think’s EnVisioning Programme the full rebrand was researched, concepted, named, explained and designed within a remarkable 6 weeks. What marked this project apart were the lengths required to go to in gathering both internal and external (customer, supplier and prospective customer) perceptions, mapping not only their value perceptions but their comparable market-wide perceptions. From that insightful understanding our process evolved to form the brand vision, values and voice the brand name and visual identity should distinctively represent.

Choosing from a range of suited names, Vertemax was selected on the strength of the name’s aptness, distinctiveness and trademark availability.

Key components to the brand journey were formally introducing new messaging, including the principle marketing use of ‘workzone safety’ referring to vulnerable work sites, such as construction or other industrial sites, where safety must remain increasingly safety focused. Stepping up to the challenge of informing customers, and the market at large, a campaign was forged spanning direct marketing, social media, email and search marketing, trade advertising and PR, explaining the positive rationale and the new brand vision.

Complimenting this we similarly worked to a short timeframe is designing and developing a powerful web-presence. Scalable, versatile, intuitively navigable and impactful, it is also adaptable to multi-territories.

OUTCOMES
  • Renaming and Brand delivered within 6 weeks
  • Website delivered on-time within 3 months
