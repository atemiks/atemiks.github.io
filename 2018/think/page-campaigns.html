Campaigns

We are ready to help you create a campaign that delivers the results you want, whether omni-channel, traditional or a focused digital campaign.

FEATURED CAMPAIGN PROJECT

We draw on the ready expertise throughout the agency to build campaign teams for each client, matching creative and technical skillsets, from copywriting to design to campaign managers.

GETTING STARTED

The first step is to create that compelling campaign creative – the idea.

We’ll work to understand your customer

  • What will be important to them in thinking about your offering?
  • Where we can reach them?
  • What’s the right time and place?

With the outcome in mind, we’ll advise you as to the effectiveness of desirable channels, individual or multi-channel, such as:

  • Traditional: Newspaper, Magazine, Ambient, Brochures…
  • Digital: Email, Pay-Per-Click, Display, Video, Social Media, Affiliate…
  • Broadcast: TV, Radio, Cinema…

Taking the creative to the media, our technical experts take over in delivering the campaign, meeting any ad booking schedule or pairing the creative with the digital channel, such as Facebook, AdWords.



RUNNING YOUR CAMPAIGN

Monitoring your campaign every step of the way, your campaign manager will monitor its delivery and performance and continue to optimise the campaign.

We’ll keep you updated to a schedule which suits you beyond planned milestone meetings.



MEASUREMENTS & METRICS

We will work with you to setup a measurement system, charting trackable hard and soft metric, from conversions to visibility.
