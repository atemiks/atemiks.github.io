Only get notifications when you have time to fill. Have a free evening or afternoon coming up? Get notified about new events within a time range you set.

Color codes are assigned to different groups of interests. The colored rings around profile pictures give you an idea of how similar your interests are.

Typed in just like hashtags, interest tags show what you’re really into, making every profile unique — as it should be!
