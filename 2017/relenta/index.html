What is Customer Interaction Management (CIM)?

It’s a new discipline comprised of the different way of thinking and a set of tools.

CIM is a way to solve the three key problems that undermine the efficiency of your interactions:

Your team is drowning in information, emails, texts and social media messages. CIM provides the resources to claim your focus back/give you a piece of mind.

Interruptions are everywhere. They interfere with your life, work and customer relationships. Prevent interruptions and downtime with CIM training.
