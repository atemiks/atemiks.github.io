dell’Azienda Agricola: House Plan 4839

This four-bedroom farm house design offers the added benefit of outdoor living areas. The home’s 4,839 sq. ft. of interior living space will impress you as well designed and sensible. A front porch and a rear lanai allow you to enjoy fresh air while relaxing or entertaining. The dramatic foyer gives way to a showpiece piano room with a curved staircase. The island kitchen serves any meal with friendliness and style. A fireplace adds ambience to the spacious family room, which hosts any gathering quite comfortably. The luxurious master suite boasts posh amenities and direct access to a safe room for storm protection. A quiet study would be great for business or hobbies. Have a look at the three bedrooms and three baths on the upper level, along with an oversized game room
